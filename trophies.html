UCL

Liverpool FC most recently won the UEFA Champions League (UCL) in 2019. They defeated Tottenham Hotspur 2-0 in the final held on June 1, 2019, at the Estadio Metropolitano in Madrid. Mohamed Salah scored a penalty in the second minute, and Divock Origi added a late goal to secure the victory.

CWC

Liverpool FC most recently won the FIFA Club World Cup in 2019. They defeated Flamengo 1-0 in the final, held on December 21, 2019, at the Khalifa International Stadium in Qatar. Roberto Firmino scored the decisive goal in extra time, securing Liverpool's first Club World Cup title.
